Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Welcome to StoryLives! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your information when you use our mobile application.

StoryLives offers premium features through subscription plans. When you purchase a subscription:

  • Subscription status and purchase history are stored securely

You can exercise these rights by contacting us at: storylivesapp@gmail.com
