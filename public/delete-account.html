StoryLives – Account Deletion Information

You can permanently delete your StoryLives account directly from within the app. All personal data associated with your account will be removed immediately upon confirmation.

How to Delete Your Account:

  • Open the StoryLives app and log in to your account.
  • Tap the menu icon (☰) in the top-left corner of the screen.
  • Scroll down and tap “Delete Account” at the bottom of the menu.
  • Confirm your choice to permanently delete your account and associated data.

Data That Will Be Deleted:

  • Your email address
  • Profile information
  • Notification token (FCM)
  • Premium subscription status (as stored on our servers)

Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.
